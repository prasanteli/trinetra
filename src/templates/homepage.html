FAQ's

Frequenty asked questions

What is Trinetra?

Trinetra is an e-Marketplace for Govt. schemes and services. Using Trinetra, you can find various government schemes, check your eligibility, and apply for the schemes.

How Trinetra will help common citizens?

Trinetra will reduce the time and effort of citizens in searching for appropriate government schemes. The easy user interface of Trinetra helps common people a lot in discovering and applying for schemes.

Can I apply for the schemes through Trinetra?

For now, the platform shall direct you to the application page of the scheme of your choice. It is envisaged that in the upcoming phases, Trinetra shall have the feature to apply for schemes/services from within the platform/app.

How does Trinetra work? How do I check for my eligibility through Trinetra?

Trinetra offers a convenient three-step process for the citizens to discover the government schemes for which they are eligible: Step 1: The user enters his/her attributes such as demographic, income, social details, etc. Step 2: The Trinetra finds the relevant schemes from hundreds of schemes for the user. Step 3: The user can select from the list of eligible schemes and get more information from the dedicated scheme page with detailed knowledge, FAQs, and application process and traverse to the application URL to apply.

What all information about a particular scheme can I find on Trinetra?

Trinetra provides details on the eligibility criteria, application process, and benefits being offered under that scheme. Trinetra also answers the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) about the scheme.

What are the broad sectors across which the schemes are available in Trinetra?

In Trinetra, schemes & services are available across a wide range of sectors such as Agriculture, Rural & Environment, Business & Entrepreneurship, Education & Learning, Health & Wellness, Housing & Shelter, Banking, Financial Services & Insurance, Public Safety, Law & Justice, Science, IT & Communication, Skills & Employment, Social welfare & Empowerment, Sports & Culture, Tax & Revenue, Transport & Infrastructure, Travel & Tourism, Utility & Sanitation.

I want to apply for a specific scheme, but I need to know the documents that I am needed to provide to apply. Can I find this information in Trinetra?

Yes, Trinetra provides the complete list of the required documents for all the schemes.
