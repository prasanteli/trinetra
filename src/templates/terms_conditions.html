Thayi Bhagya Scheme(comprehensive Maternal Healthcare)

The scheme has been designed so that women belonging to BPL families can avail totally cashless treatment in these private hospitals./n Under this scheme, the pregnant woman belonging to BPL family can avail delivery services free of cost in the registered private hospital near her house./n She is not required to pay any charges right from the point of admission to discharge. The benefit is limited to the first two live deliveries./n The beneficiaries are identified through the ANC cards issued to them./n The scheme has been introduced in the six "C" category districts of Gulbarga, Bidar, Raichur, Koppal, Bijapur and Bagalkot and the backward district Chamarajanagar./n .
